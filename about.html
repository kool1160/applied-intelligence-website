About

After nearly twenty years of watching the same manufacturing problems get patched instead of solved, Christopher Hilton saw the real gap clearly: the fixes existed, but the system did not.

Applied Intelligence was built to close that gap through small connected systems that make issues visible, route ownership clearly, standardize response, and create real improvement momentum.
